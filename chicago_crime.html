Chicago Crime

Given that chicago is one of the deadliest cities in the USA, I wanted to understand the criminal environment in chicago over the last 20 years. The data used for this analysis is available on google cloud and can be queried using BigQuery.
For this analysis I queried the data using SQL on BigQuery, and downloaded the data as a csv file. Visuals were made on EXCEL.

Questions :
1. How has Chicago's criminal environment changed over the years?
2. Are there any types of crime that follow a particular trend over the years?
3. Is there a particular distribution of crime over the months of the year?
4. Is there one or more locations that stand out from the rest in terms of crime rates?

Hypothesis :
1. Chicago's crime environment could experience a steady rise or fall in crime over the years.
2. Certain types of crime may follow a trend over time.
3. Maybe
4. Yes

Data Analysis :
This section contains the visuals produced during data analysis.

This graph shows that Chicago's crime environment is on the decline between 2001 and 2023.

From 2001 to 2023, the crime rate dropped by more than 74%.

This graph shows the top 10 types of crime in Chicago and how they have changed over the years.

Consistent with the overall crime graph, this chart also shows a downward trend for most crime types, in particular narcotics type.

The chart shows that the top ten crime types in Chicago account for 92% of all crimes committed in Chicago.

The remaining 8% represents the other 26 crime types.

This graph shows that the monthly distribution of crime in Chicago follows no particular pattern.

There is no huge difference between the months.

Street, Residence, Apartment and Sidewalk are the four main places where most crime occurs in Chicago.
